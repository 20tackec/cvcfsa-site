About Us

The Chinese Friendship and Scholarship Association (CFSA) is a cultural organization based in Eau Claire, WI and surrounding areas. The organization is dedicated to fostering understanding and friendship within the Chinese community, as well as promote and celebrate traditional Chinese traditions through food, music, and art. Founded with the vision of unity through diversity, CFSA hosts a variety of programs that celebrate heritage, connect cultures, and build lasting friendships.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build bridges between cultures through education, collaboration, and celebration. We aim to:

  • Promote appreciation and understanding of Chinese language and culture.
  • Host events that celebrate Chinese traditions and foster community engagement.
  • Encourage lifelong friendships and community service.

What We Do

CFSA organizes cultural festivals, guest speaker events, and social gatherings throughout the year. Whether you are a student, community member, or ally, CFSA welcomes you to join us in building a supportive and vibrant multicultural community.

Some of our key activities include: Chinese New Year celebrations, Mid-Autumn Festival gatherings, Moon Festival events, and more!
