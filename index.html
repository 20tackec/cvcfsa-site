About CFSA

dedicated to fostering understanding, friendship, and celebration of Chinese traditions through food, music, and art.

Who We Are

A Community Rooted
in Heritage & Friendship

CFSA is a cultural organization based in Eau Claire, WI and the surrounding area. Founded with the vision of unity through diversity, CFSA hosts a variety of programs that celebrate heritage, connect cultures, and build lasting friendships.

一

Language & Culture

Promote appreciation and understanding of Chinese language and cultural traditions within our community.

二

Events & Celebration

Host events that celebrate Chinese traditions and foster meaningful community engagement throughout the year.

三

Community & Friendship

Encourage lifelong friendships and community service throughout the Chippewa Valley.

What We Do

A Full Calendar of
Cultural Programs

CFSA organizes cultural festivals, guest speaker events, and social gatherings throughout the year. Whether you are a student, community member, or ally, CFSA welcomes you to join us in building a supportive and vibrant multicultural community.
